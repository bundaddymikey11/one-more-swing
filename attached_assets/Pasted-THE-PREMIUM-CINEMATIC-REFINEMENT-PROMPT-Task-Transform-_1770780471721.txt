THE "PREMIUM CINEMATIC" REFINEMENT PROMPT
Task: Transform the current site into a world-class, cinematic digital experience. Focus on "High-End Motion" and "Editorial Layout."

1. Typography & Hierarchy (The Editorial Look):

Headlines: Use a "Display" font style. Set letter-spacing: -0.05em and line-height: 0.9. Use "Fluid Typography" so headlines are massive but perfectly scaled on mobile.

Contrast: Use high-contrast sizing—giant headlines paired with tiny, wide-spaced sub-labels (e.g., text-xs uppercase tracking-[0.3em]).

2. Advanced Motion & Transitions (The "Mind-Blow" Factor):

Parallax Scroll: Implement a subtle parallax effect on background images so they move slower than the foreground text.

Reveal Animations: Use Framer Motion to create "Staggered Reveals." Don't just fade in; have lines of text "mask-up" (sliding up from an invisible line) like a premium movie trailer.

Smooth Scroll: Enable a "Lenis" or "Smooth Scroll" behavior to give the entire site a silky, weighted movement.

3. Creative Layout (Breaking the Grid):

Asymmetry: Shift some elements slightly off-center. Use overlapping layers (e.g., a high-res photo of a golf ball partially overlapping a text block).

Whitespace: Triple the current vertical spacing (py-40 or py-56). Luxury is the luxury of space.

Background Depth: Add a subtle, dark "noise" or "grain" texture overlay to the entire site to remove the "flat AI" look and give it a tactile, filmic quality.

4. High-End Mobile Specifics:

One-Handed Navigation: Ensure the most important button (Inquire) is always pinned or easily reachable at the bottom of the mobile viewport in a "Floating Action Button" style.

Haptic Feel: Ensure all buttons have a scale-down effect (0.95) when tapped to mimic a physical button press.

5. Luxury "Details" Section:

Create a "Features" section that uses high-res imagery and "Hotspots." As the user scrolls, have the specs (Garmin, 4K, Titleist) appear as elegant floating labels over a moody image of the simulator.
